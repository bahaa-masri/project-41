About Us

Founded in 1966 - Fernand Hosri Group Holding

We are a multinational enterprise active throughout the MENA region, with offices located in Lebanon, UAE, and KSA.
Our reach spans local and global markets, with expertise in Telecom, Security, Automation, Communication, Renewable Energy, FMCG Retail and Distribution, Office Supplies and Equipment, Insurance Brokerage and Consultancy, Fire Safety, and Property Management.
Across every sector, we stay true to one promise: Quality First.
